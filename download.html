Download Ajax Live Search

This section explains how to download both the free and premium versions of this plugin.

Installation

There are two versions of Ajax Live Search that you can install; the light version and the premium version.

Lite Version

  • Login into your WordPress admin dashboard.
  • On the sidebar, click plugins and then click add new.
  • On the next page, type Ajax Live Search then search.
  • From the results; locate the one that has a green icon and has Picocodes as the author, then click install
  • After it installs; click activate and you are good to go!

Premium Version

If you need the increased speed and functionality that comes with the premium version; you will first have to buy a license (licenses start at $29).

After payment confirmation, you will recieve a download link for your purchase. Use it to download the plugin and then follow these steps to install it.

Setting up the environment

Before you can start searching, Ajax Live Search will first need to index your posts. During this process, your site may start slowing down but everything will go back to normal as soon as everything becomes indexed.

However; there are a couple of things you can do to speed up this process.

  1. First of all, increase your memory limit and maximum excecution time to x3 the current amount.
  2. Then, on the Ajax Live Search settings page, click on the indexing tab and then increase the value of batches to a higher number (Preferably 1/5 of the total number of your posts).
  3. Finally, continue browsing your site and after a few minutes all your posts should be indexed. set everything back to their initial values and go on to the next section.
